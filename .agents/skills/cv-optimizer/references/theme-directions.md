# Theme Directions

Use these patterns when `--mode visual` or `--mode full` requires style guidance.

## Conservative

Best for: Traditional employers, highly formal contexts.

- Typography: neutral sans-serif stack with clear hierarchy.
- Color: dark neutral text with minimal accent.
- Layout: single column, strict rhythm, low ornament.
- Signal: reliability, clarity, low-risk professionalism.

## Modern-Tech

Best for: International product companies, scale-ups, platform roles.

- Typography: contemporary sans-serif pairing with strong section labels.
- Color: restrained contrast accent, neutral base.
- Layout: single-column content with sharp spacing grid.
- Signal: technical clarity and current market alignment.

## Premium

Best for: Staff or lead roles with high ownership and cross-functional scope.

- Typography: editorial hierarchy with balanced density.
- Color: deep neutral palette with subtle accent.
- Layout: disciplined whitespace, strong scanability, print-safe.
- Signal: seniority, confidence, and narrative control.

## Required Implementation Mapping

Always provide:

- Type scale and heading sizes.
- Margin and spacing rhythm.
- Section divider style and density.
- Accent strategy and contrast considerations.
- Print and ATS safety notes.
