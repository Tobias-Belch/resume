# Review Checklists

## Content Checklist

- Summary states seniority, scope, outcomes, and target direction.
- Bullets use action-impact-metric structure where possible.
- Passive, generic wording is reduced.
- Skills listed are defensible in interview context.
- JD-critical terms appear naturally when tailoring is enabled.

## Structure Checklist

- Section headings are standard and parseable.
- Chronology is reverse and date formats are consistent.
- Recent 10-12 years receive most detail.
- Older roles are compressed without losing continuity.
- Layout recommendations avoid ATS-hostile constructs.

## Visual Checklist

- Visual direction fits employer and role context.
- Hierarchy is clear and legible in print.
- Typography and spacing are explicit and practical.
- Accent usage is restrained and professional.
- Visual recommendations remain machine-readable.

## Tailoring Checklist

- Title and core terminology align with target role.
- Tailoring depth matches `light` or `mirror` mode.
- Mirrored terms are backed by CV evidence.
- Language output matches configured language policy.
- No claim inflation is introduced.
